The Best Movies According to Me:

My Top 3 Movies of All Time:

Rise of Ottomans: The Conquest of Constantinople

Its a story about the siege laid by the Ottoman king, Mehmud II to capture the city of Constatinople, which was then known as the Crossroads of the World.

Fall of Troy

It's a story about a prince of Troy who got a boon from the love goddess Aphrodite that he would marry the most beautiful woman in all of Greece. That woman happened to be a queen of kingdom in Greece. Abducting her and taking her to Troy, the prince faced the wrath of the mighty Greek warriors like Agammennon,Achilles and others which ultimately led to the destruction of the city, Troy.

Girl With A disease

Its a love story between a girl who has a traumatic past along with Alzheimer's disease, who tends to forget every recent memory of her's and a poor boy who has a lot of struggles in life but has a dream to become the greatest singer of the world.
